About BorrowMe

BorrowMe is a peer-to-peer lending platform, starting at HBS. Our initial focus is on board games, so you and your section mates can bond over your favorites or try something new! See below for games available to rent at just $5/night - just text number with your request (include game and date) and we'll arrange a time and place for you to pick it up!
